Vastu Shastra is a traditional Indian belief system about spatial orientation, not a scientific or structural standard. These directional readings are offered for cultural and reference purposes — for construction or renovation decisions, consult a licensed architect or engineer, and a Vastu consultant if you wish to follow this tradition. Phone compasses can be thrown off by nearby metal, magnets, or magnetic phone cases — hold your device flat, away from these, and calibrate it (moving it in a figure-8) before taking a reading.
