Foundations

Vastu Shastra Explained

People talk about a room's "vibe" without knowing there's a science behind it. Vastu Shastra is that science — an ancient framework for building homes and workspaces that channel positive energy instead of blocking it. It's why the practice still guides the construction of houses, offices, and temples alike.

The word itself splits in two: Vastu means dwelling, Shastra means science. Together they describe a discipline for balancing the five elements — earth, water, fire, air, and space — across a building's layout. A living room angled toward the east to greet the morning sun is one small example of a much larger system.
